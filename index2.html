About Us
Saba Peel Fertilizer

BNN Naturals is a production and manufacturing company, producing high quality products made from different varities of bananas, helping the environment with waste pollution. BNN Naturals was created by 11 talented researchers, aiming to help the environment and make use of the banana waste. This company was established on September 2024, lead by President Glaiza Sheryl C. Espina and her chief officers. The company's vision is to be one of the prominent manufacturing company in agricultural industry, providing good quality products and services for their customers and comfortable environment for their employees. The company's mission is to turn banana and its waste into a better and effective product, produce affordable and high quality goods, and serves the customer with care. BNN Naturals have Triple A Values which stands for Authentic, Adaptable, and Available. The products are authentic and gives for we make sure that our product will under go quality control and have visible marks like logos and stickers. Adaptable for our company makes sure that our inventions and capabilities can adapt to technology, and the needs of the people. Our products are made sure to be available and is aligned to the supply and demand of the costumers. As our tagline goes, "we care for the environment, we care for your health".
